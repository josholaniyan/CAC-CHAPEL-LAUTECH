HISTORY...
Dunamis International Gospel Centre is a dynamic Pentecostal Christian Church founded by the man of God – Pastor Dr. Paul Enenche. It is a big family of power, glory and the unfathomable flow of God’s blessings. God continues to increase the Dunamis International Gospel Centre family, even as he continues to show his mighty works through our founder – Pastor Dr. Paul Eneche. We thank God for our founder’s obedience to heaven’s call. One singular act of obedience has undoubtedly made the Dunamis International Gospel Centre a source of tremendous blessing to millions of people around the world. Upon God’s divine instruction, Pastor Dr. Paul Eneche got the directive to start this ministry and also the name to give it. He named the ministry “Dunamis International Gospel Centre” in total obedience to the word of God. Dunamis International Gospel Centre was founded on the 10th of November 1996. Hence, the Kingdom Power and Glory World Conference, which takes place every November, is an annual anniversary conference where the Dunamis family, sons of the ministry and many others gather annually.
